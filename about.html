About me

Hello, my name is Natalie Vos. I am a Lithuanian portrait photographer from Klaipeda, Lithuania. I love sunrises in the mornings, travel, and dinner parties. I am a passionate person, and one of my main goals in life is to bring joy to people, travel around the world, and create great connections with amazing people.
